About Me

I am a Postdoc at EPFL's Realistic Graphics Lab (RGL), led by Prof. Wenzel Jakob. I earned my PhD in Computer Science from Cornell University, where I was advised by Prof. Steve Marschner. My research primarily focuses on physically based rendering and my PhD thesis is about rendering of complex materials using wave optics. Prior to pursuing graduate studies, I received my BS in Applied Mathematics from University of California, Los Angeles (UCLA). Outside work, I enjoy spending time hiking and rock climbing.
