About Me

I am a Postdoc at the Realistic Graphics Lab (RGL) at École Polytechnique Fédérale de Lausanne (EPFL), advised by Prof. Wenzel Jakob. I earned my PhD in Computer Science from Cornell University in 2022, where I was advised by Prof. Steve Marschner. My research primarily focuses on physically based rendering and my PhD thesis is about rendering complex materials using wave optics. Prior to pursuing graduate studies, I received my Bachelor of Science in Applied Mathematics from University of California, Los Angeles (UCLA) in 2016, where I worked with Prof. Dominique Zosso, Prof. Chenfanfu Jiang, and Prof. Joseph Teran.

Outside work, I enjoy spending time hiking and rock climbing.
